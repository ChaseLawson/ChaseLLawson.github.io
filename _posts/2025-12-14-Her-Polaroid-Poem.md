## Her Polaroid 

Grief hits in waves
Her father’s right hook
Came from the closet
After his death 
In a Reebok shoebox
Adjacent to his bed 

Photos of him 
With various women 
That weren’t her mother 
All posing in 
Bikinis
At tropical beaches 

Somewhere hot like Florida.
