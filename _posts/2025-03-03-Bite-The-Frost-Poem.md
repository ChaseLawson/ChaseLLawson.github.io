## Bite The Frost

I watch my breath rise 
And dissolve,, offering  
To the season that holds  
What is gone..  
Now everything is still--
Not empty-- 
But waiting.. 
The warmth of your hand  
Gathering what was ripe,, 
Letting the rest fall 
As the air remembers 
The weight of bees..
